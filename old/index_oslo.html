Hirokazu Nasu

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the department of mathematics at the University of Oslo in Norway.

Education

I completed my Ph.D. at the RIMS, Kyoto University in 2005 under the supervision of Prof. Shigeru Mukai. Before coming to Kyoto, I spent four years as a graduate student at the Graduate School of Mathematics in the Nagoya University.
